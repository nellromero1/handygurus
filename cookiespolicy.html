Managing cookies

Handy Guru’s website is basically informational with no data collected for transactional processing. Our website allows our customers and prospects to learn about the services we offer. On our To Contact US page, we may collect certain personally identifiable information and during a chat conversation (initiated within our website) we preserve the logs of the chat exchanges between prospects, customers, potential employees, etc. In general, any individual can visit our website without revealing any personally identifiable information but as this information is revealed we take the appropriate provisions to protect it.

Typically, when by visiting a website, IP addresses are collected and logged as part of the process; however, we do not link any IP addresses to any personally identifiable information, therefore our visitors will remain anonymous to us unless they initiate a chat session.

Every customer or site visitor can manage cookies by using features and functions available on most Internet browsers. For example, most browsers will allow you to choose what cookies can be placed on your computer and to delete or disable cookies. You can find instructions for managing cookie controls on websites for browsers within the Settings/Internet Options areas for Microsoft Internet Explorer browsers, Apple Safari browsers, Google Chrome, and Mozilla Firefox browsers.
